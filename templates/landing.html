About Us
Built for Smarter Farming

GreenGrow is an AI-powered fertilizer recommendation system developed to help farmers make smarter agricultural decisions. By combining soil nutrient data, crop type, and real-time weather conditions, our system provides accurate fertilizer guidance to improve yield and reduce environmental impact.

Our goal is to promote sustainable farming practices by minimizing excessive fertilizer usage while ensuring crops receive the right nutrients at the right time.
